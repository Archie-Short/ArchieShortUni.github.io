About Me

I'm a c++ / python programmer currently in their final year studying a masters in computer science with games technology. From Dorset but now living in Brighton, I'm looking to find work in the industry and learn as much as I can to create fun and impactful experiences. I have a passion for not just games but the community around them, I volunteered at last years GDLX and am the Grads in Games ambassador for City University of London.

Outside of games I enjoy Archery and climbing as well as regularly running Dungeons and Dragons sessions for my friends and beginners around Brighton.
